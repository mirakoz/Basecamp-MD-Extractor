### Month 16 - March 2026 MPR

Posted by Mirak Ozoglu on Apr 13

[![Mirak Ozoglu](https://asset1.basecamp.com/2325709/people/19154717/photo/avatar.96.gif "Mirak Ozoglu")](/2325709/people/19154717)

Hi Mike,

Thank you for your patience! Your March PPC Performance Report for Jewish Family Services is now ready.

[https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1xxqhPPaxu-8sxQ7TDD6zQoI4wyhWdo2mVCC…](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1xxqhPPaxu-8sxQ7TDD6zQoI4wyhWdo2mVCCqfTDnXm4/edit?usp=sharing "https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1xxqhPPaxu-8sxQ7TDD6zQoI4wyhWdo2mVCCqfTDnXm4/edit?usp=sharing")

Here are the key combined results from March (reflecting data from our reactivation on March 10th through the end of the month):

* **Overall Spend:** $1,997.24
* **Total Conversions:** 22 leads
* **Cost per Lead:** $90.78
* **Combined Conversion Rate:** 3.81%

**Monthly Insights & Actions:**

* **Platform Efficiency:** Google Ads drove excellent cost efficiency this month, generating 17 leads at a low Cost per Lead of $59.08.
* **Brand Awareness:** Meta Ads heavily outpaced Google in sheer visibility, generating over 23,317 impressions and helping us push the new visual branding to a wider audience.
* **Reactivation:** Considering campaigns were paused in February and only ran for about three weeks in March, generating 22 total leads shows that the account rebounded quickly with strong demand.

**What's Completed in the Previous Month?**

* Successfully reactivated all Google and Meta campaigns on March 10th.

**What's Going to be done in the Next Month?**

* Replaced legacy creatives with the new "sample experience" videos (Grounding & Progressive Muscle Relaxation) on Meta.
* Updated ad copy and captions to accurately reflect Medicaid acceptance and flexible payment options.

Please let us know if you have any questions or if you and Ryan would like to schedule a quick call to discuss the report and the new video performance in more detail. We're always here to help!
