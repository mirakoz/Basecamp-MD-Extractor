**What are the main insights for the month?**

In May, our Google Ads campaigns generated a total of 6 phone calls and 10 form submissions, resulting in an overall conversion rate of 8.08% and a cost per conversion of $92.70. The click-through rate increased from 4.33% to 4.90%.

At the campaign level, the Generic Counseling campaign generated 10 conversions. Campaign's conversion rate increased from 5.94% to 10.99%.

In Meta Ads, we completed the month with 18 conversions.  The cost per lead decreased from $54.41 to $24.58. The conversion rate increased from 3.57% to 9.38%. Overall, the campaign demonstrated positive performance metrics, highlighting the effectiveness.

**What did we do during the month?**

* Implemented bid and budget adjustments.
* Added new negative keywords based on search terms.

**Recommendations / Requests**

* Lead quality feedback is very important for campaign optimization.
* New videos and images are required to refresh ad creatives and maintain engagement for Meta Ads.
