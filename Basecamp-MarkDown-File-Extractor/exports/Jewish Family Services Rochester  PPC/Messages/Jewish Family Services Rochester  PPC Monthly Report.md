The Aging Counseling campaign had the highest click-through rate at 9.64%. The keywords that brought the most traffic were related to mental health counseling.   
**What did we do during the month?**  

* We conducted an experiment for the Generic Counseling campaign.

* Negative keywords have been added based on search term results.
