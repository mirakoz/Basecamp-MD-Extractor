**What are the main insights for the month?**  
  

The performance of our Google Ads in February showed a significant improvement compared to January. We completed the month with 13 conversions, which included 7 form submissions and 6 phone calls.

The conversion rate saw an impressive increase of 83% compared to the previous month, and we achieved the lowest cost per conversion at $110 in February.

At the campaign level, the best performing campaign was the Generic Counseling campaign, with a 9.52% conversion rate. Additionally, 2 conversions came from the Competitor campaign, and 2 conversions came from the Couple Counseling campaign.

The keyword that generated the most conversions was "mental health counseling".

In Meta Ads, the campaigns were launched for only 4 days in February. During this time, we spent $52 and received 1 conversion. The conversion rate was 2.38%, and the click-through rate was 1.24%.   
  
  
**What did we do during the month?**  

* Meta Ads campaign was launched.
* The Search - Competitor campaign was launched.

* The Search - Child & Adolescent campaign bid strategy has been updated from manual CPC to maximum conversions.

* Looker Studio Live Report has been updated to include Meta Ads results.
