 **What are the main insights for the month?**

The performance of our Google Ads in March showed a significant improvement compared to February. We completed the month with 21 conversions, which included 9 form submissions and 12 phone calls.  
The conversion rate saw an impressive increase of 51% compared to the previous month, and we achieved our lowest cost per conversion at $68.37.  
The click-through rate increased from 4.08% to 4.45%.  
  
At the campaign level, the best-performing campaign was the Generic Counseling campaign, which had a conversion rate of 13.64%. We also recorded 7 conversions from the Competitor campaign, 1 conversion from the Couple Counseling campaign, and 1 conversion from the Couple Counseling Experiment campaign.  
  
The keyword that generated the most conversions was "mental health rochester ny ".

In Meta Ads, we generated 10 conversions with a 4.44% conversion rate.

**What did we do during the month?**

* We conducted an experiment on bidding strategies for the Couple Counseling campaign to improve performance.
* Implemented bid and budget adjustments.
* Added new negative keywords based on search terms.
