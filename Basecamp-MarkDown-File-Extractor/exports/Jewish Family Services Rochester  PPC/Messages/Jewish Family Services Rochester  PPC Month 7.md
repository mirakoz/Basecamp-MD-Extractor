**What are the main insights for the month?**

In April, our Google Ads campaigns generated a total of 4 phone calls and 12 form submissions, resulting in an overall conversion rate of 8.25% and a cost per conversion of $93.44.

At the campaign level, the Generic Counseling campaign generated 6 conversions, achieving a conversion rate of 5.94%.

In Meta Ads, the campaign generated 8 leads in April. Click-through rate (CTR) increased from 0.89% to 1.00%. The cost per click (CPC) decreased from $1.98 to $1.94. Overall, the campaign demonstrated positive performance metrics, highlighting the effectiveness.

**What did we do during the month?**

* Added new, high-potential keyword based on insights from the search terms report.
* We excluded some locations due to low performance.

**Recommendations / Requests**

* Lead quality feedback is very important for campaign optimization.
* New videos and images are required to refresh ad creatives and maintain engagement for Meta Ads.
