**Here are some key results from November.**Overall Spend: $1,035.26  
CTR: 1.88%  
CPC: $7.19  
Conversion Rate: 6.94%  
  
**Monthly Insights & Actions:**  
  

Following the location change, the campaigns began a new optimization phase, and we’re already seeing positive results.

* Conversion rate increased from 4.10% to 6.94%, showing improved traffic quality.
* We completed the month with 10 conversions, which included 10 calls from ads.
* The Wealth Management campaign achieved the highest conversion rate at 8.77%, standing out as the top performer.
* The Retirement Planning campaign showed a strong recovery this month. While it did not generate any conversions in October, it performed significantly better in November, resulting in 4 conversions.

  
**What's Completed in the Previous Month?**  

* We added 10 negative keywords.
* We had 30 changes on the Google Ads account.

Yeliz
