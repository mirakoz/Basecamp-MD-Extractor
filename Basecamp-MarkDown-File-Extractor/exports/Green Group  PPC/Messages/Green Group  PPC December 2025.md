**Here are some key results from December.**Overall Spend: $940  
CTR: 2.38%  
CPC: $6.40  
Conversion Rate: 5.44%  
  
**Monthly Insights & Actions:**  

* The click-through rate improved from 1.88% to 2.38%, indicating stronger ad relevance and user engagement.
* At the same time, the cost per click decreased from $7.19 to $6.40, showing increased efficiency in traffic acquisition.
* While the cost per conversion has increased, the leads generated this period are of higher quality compared to previous months, making the overall performance more valuable for the business.
* The highest conversion rate belonged to the Wealth Management campaign with 7.89%.

  The Wealth Management campaign showed a strong recovery this month. The campaign’s cost per conversion decreased from $94.91 to $74.04 in December.

  
**What's Completed in the Previous Month?**  

* We had 21 changes on the Google Ads account.
* We added 13 negative keywords.
