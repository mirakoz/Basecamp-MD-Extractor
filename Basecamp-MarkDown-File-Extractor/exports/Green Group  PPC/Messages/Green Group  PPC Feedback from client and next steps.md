Posted by Francisco Freyre on Jul 21, 2023

[![Francisco Freyre](https://asset1.basecamp.com/2325709/people/18046896/photo/avatar.96.gif "Francisco Freyre")](/2325709/people/18046896)

Hello Yigit, sorry that I just respond after the meeting with the client.   
  
After reviewing the recommendations made to avoid the conflicts with the iframe, they came back with what I think is good news. They were granted access to their main website <https://www.greengrouppwm.com/>  
So now we will be able to build the landing page in this main website and we will be able to better showcase their products.   
  
With this, they are now asking if it is worth to keep the webinar as the center of the campaign or if we could focus more in the products and have the webinar access in the landing page as a plus.   
From the way we structured the campaign, I think there are several adds promoting the services (Tax planning and Retirement planning) rather than the Free Webinar. If I'm correct, perhaps is just a matter of how to structure the landing page rather than the campaign.   
  
They were thinking to pause the campaign while we do the changes as they have not received a lot of conversions, but I told them that, besides all the issues with the webinar application they are using, it is also that we have been with the campaign active for 1 month, so they agreed to keep it going while we do the new landing page, do you think this is the best thing to do or shall we pause it as to hey wanted?  
  
Finally, they got also access to their Google My Business and they are managing it. They received the message from Google that if they link the campaign their Google My Business they can get a ~$700 credit to their ads account. Is this something we have to do or how can we use those funds for their campaign?  
  
Thanks and regards

**Yigit Gok**   
Hi Francisco,  
  
That seems like good news for us. This means we'll be able to provide a better user experience to visitors and collect a variety of conversions data.  
  
As for the webinar, I believe It's a good way of collecting leads. It acts as a lead magnet. Which means users will fill out their information in order to reach the next step (webinar). However, due to the poor performance of the webinar video player, the iframe tracking problem and lack of different webinar options, I believe It would be ideal to either use a different sort of lead magnet (PDF Download, research page, consultation or at least a working webinar page) or simply have people fill out a lead form.  
  
Before deciding what to do with the active campaigns, Is there any chance for us to find out the number of lead form submissions they received? Additionally, learning about phone calls and booking consultations would also work. This way, we can have an informed decision about the campaigns.  
  
If you can provide me with an email from client's side, I can invite them to our Google Ads account. Then they can link their Google My Business account to Google Ads. Alternatively, they can give us Admin/Owner access on their Google My Business account and we can do the linking.  
  
All the best,  
Yigit
