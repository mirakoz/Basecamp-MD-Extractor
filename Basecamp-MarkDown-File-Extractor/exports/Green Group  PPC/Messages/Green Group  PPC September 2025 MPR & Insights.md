**Here are some key results from September.**  

Overall Spend: $1,044  
We completed the month with 32 conversions, which included 4 lead form submissions,15 phone number clicks from the website, 2 calls from ads, and 11 email clicks.  
Retirement Planning campaign’s CTR increased from 7.21% to 9.41%   
The highest conversion rate belonged to the Retirement Planning campaign with 2.66%.

**Monthly Insights & Actions:**

* The number of lead form conversions remained the same compared to last month. However, since email click conversions dropped from 43 to 11, our overall results were negatively affected.

* Additionally, the seasonal decline in the tax planning campaign is also affecting our results.
* We had 21 changes on the Google Ads account.
* We added 5 negative keywords.
