### Monthly Report & Insights - March 2025

Posted by Yeliz Kolasinli on Apr 9, 2025

[![Yeliz Kolasinli](https://asset1.basecamp.com/2325709/people/18747394/photo/avatar.96.gif "Yeliz Kolasinli")](/2325709/people/18747394)

Hi Francisco,  
  
You can find the link to our monthly report for March 2025 below;  
  
<https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1ImxxMXI2X1Y7b8vgHEwlCt0fI_KpT9ZP8lcaKWTxq4s/edit?slide=id.g127c65e30f2_0_0#slide=id.g127c65e30f2_0_0>  
  
**What are the main insights for the month?**

In March, our campaign achieved notable results, we completed the month with 21 conversions, which included 15 form submissions, 20 phone calls from the website, 1 call from ads, and 22 email clicks.

We achieved an incredible milestone this month, reaching the highest conversion rate of 6.14%. Cost per Click (CPC) decreased from $1.63 to $1.15, increasing overall campaign efficiency. Additionally, the cost per conversion significantly improved, dropping from $31.11 to $18.75.

At the campaign level, the Search - Tax Planning campaign generated the highest number of conversions with 32 in total, and a strong conversion rate of 4.51%. Search - Wealth Management campaign had the highest click-through rate(CTR) with 11.44%. The Search - Retirement Planning campaign demonstrated significant cost-efficiency improvements, with the cost per conversion decreasing from $51.62 to $24.90.

**What did we do during the month?**

* Reallocated budgets toward better-performing campaigns and keywords.

* Continuously monitored search terms and updated the negative keyword list to eliminate irrelevant traffic and improve campaign efficiency.

**What's Next?**

We will prepare a report on the search terms that led to conversions for your review.  
  
  
Best regards,  
Yeliz
