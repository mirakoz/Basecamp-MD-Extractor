**What are the main insights for the month?**  
  

The performance of our Google Ads in February showed a significant improvement. We completed the month with a total of 35 leads,  which included 3 form submissions and 32 phone calls generated from our website through Google Ads.

We achieved our highest conversion rate with 5.25% in February.

Additionally, our unit cost decreased by 10.75%, while the conversion rate increased by 35%.

At the campaign level, the best-performing campaign was the Tax Planning campaign  which achieved an impressive conversion rate of 9.69%. The Wealth Management campaign also performed well, generating 8 conversions and recording the highest conversion rate with 5.59% in February compared to previous months.  
  
  
**What did we do during the month?**  

* The Landing Page has been updated.
* The bid strategy and budget were optimized.
* The negative keyword list was updated.
