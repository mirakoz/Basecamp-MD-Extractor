[https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1CKagmDTdicuVZm7rXg3MuAkc3W0fyRqQUzj…](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1CKagmDTdicuVZm7rXg3MuAkc3W0fyRqQUzj2UGeEKqU/edit#slide=id.g1b6180d6897_0_10 "https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1CKagmDTdicuVZm7rXg3MuAkc3W0fyRqQUzj2UGeEKqU/edit#slide=id.g1b6180d6897_0_10")  
  
October's performance showed improvement compared to September's results.  

* We completed the month with 42 all leads from Google Ads, which included 3 form submissions, 4 calls from ads, 30 phone calls, and 5 requests for directions.

* The average click-through rate (CTR) improved significantly, increasing by 42.2% compared to the previous month.

* Additionally, the conversion rate increased from 2.35% to 2.66% in October, and the cost per conversion decreased by 8%.

  
All the best,
