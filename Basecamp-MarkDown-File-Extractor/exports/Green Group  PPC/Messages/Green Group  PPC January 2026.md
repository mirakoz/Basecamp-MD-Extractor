**Here are some key results from January.**Overall Spend: $973.95  
CTR: 1.81%  
CPC: $6.45  
Conversion Rate: 3.97%  
  
**Monthly Insights & Actions:**

* Impressions increased by 35%, showing that ads were still eligible and visible in auctions.
* We completed the month with 6 conversions, which included 5 calls from ads and 1 form submission.
* The highest conversion rate belonged to the Wealth Management campaign with 7.41%.

  
**What's Completed in the Previous Month?**

* We had 50 changes on the Google Ads account.
* We added 10 negative keywords.
* Due to the decline in results, changes were made to the campaign structure and keyword strategy in order to target a higher-intent and higher-quality audience.
* Launched the Generic - Consultation campaign on January 29th.
