The performance of our Google Ads in September showed a significant improvement compared to August. We generated a total of 39 leads from the ads including 7 form submissions, 3 call from ads, 25 phone calls, and 4 directions.The most successful campaign was "search\_tax\_planning\_new," which resulted in 28 leads and a 9% conversion rate. Additionally, the cost per click (CPC) decreased by 21% and the cost per conversion decreased by 57%. The conversion rate increased by 86% compared to August.
