**Here are some key results from February.**Overall Spend: $1,117

CTR: 2.07%  
CPC: $7.11  
Conversion Rate: 5.10%  
  
**Monthly Insights & Actions:**  
In February, we saw a positive shift in efficiency compared to January. While impressions decreased by 9%, overall engagement improved, CTR increased from 1.81% to 2.07%, indicating stronger ad relevance and user interest.

Most importantly, lead volume increased by 33% (from 6 to 8 leads) despite similar traffic levels. This was driven by a significant improvement in conversion rate, which rose from 3.97% to 5.10% (+28%).

On the cost side, CPC increased by 10% ($6.45 → $7.11), reflecting continued auction competitiveness. However, due to the stronger conversion rate, Cost per Lead improved by 14%, decreasing from $162 to $139.

Overall, February shows that performance is stabilizing and efficiency is improving compared to January.  
  
**What's Completed in the Previous Month?**

* We had 49 changes on the Google Ads account.
* We added 35 negative keywords.
