**Here are some key results from September.**

Overall Spend: $8,208  
Total Conversions:  276 conversions at a CPA of $29.74  
Conversion rate increased from 7.68% to 8.34%  
  
**Monthly Insights & Actions:**

  

* Google Ads campaigns saw improved performance this month. Ongoing efforts in detailed targeting are helping maintain ad competitiveness despite intense market competition.

* Meta Ads performance saw a minor decrease compared to the previous month. We recommend refreshing ad creatives to maintain competitiveness.

* Primary lead rate increased from 6.32% to 8.06% compared to the previous month.
* Glenmere campaign had the highest conversion rate with 12.50%.
* Friendly Home campaign had the lowest cost per lead with $43.06.

**What's Completed in the Previous Month?**

* We had 48 changes on the Google Ads account.
* We added 31 negative keywords.
* We added 5 new keywords to the campaigns.
