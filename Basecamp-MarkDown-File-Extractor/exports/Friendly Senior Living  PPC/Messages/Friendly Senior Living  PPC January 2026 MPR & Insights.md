**Here are some key results from January.**Overall Spend: $8,509

Overall CPC: $2.43

Overall CTR: 3.32%

Total Conversions: 265 conversions at a CPA of $32.11  
  
**Monthly Insights & Actions:**

* The overall avg. CPC decreased from $2.75 to $2.43.
* The overall cost per lead remained almost the same as the previous month.
* In Google Ads, the average CPC decreased by 9% compared to the previous month, and the cost per lead decreased from $63.41 to $59.47.
* In Meta Ads, the cost per lead decreased by 3.43% compared to the previous month.
* The majority of the leads came from the Friendly Home campaign.
* The Lily Brook - Open House campaign launched on January 30th. The campaign generated 3 leads.

**What's Completed in the Previous Month?**

* We had 18 changes on the Google Ads account.
* We added 19 negative keywords.

  
Please let us know if you’d like to discuss the report, we can schedule a meeting.

**Armando Alas**   

Hi Mike,

Following our discussion regarding a lower monthly ad spend for your Google Ads campaigns, I met with Can to review the strategy.

Based on the January report, the Friendly Home and Cloverwood campaigns show high activity. For the remaining campaigns, we recommend lowering the ad spend to $200 USD per month rather than turning them off entirely.

Disabling these campaigns would cause a loss in market share visibility and could negatively impact direct and organic traffic over the long term. Furthermore, turning them off may increase future conversion costs and delay machine learning, as restarting them later would require a 3-4 week ramp-up period. Keeping them active at a lower budget allows Google to continue learning and generating results.

I have scheduled a 20-30 minute meeting in your calendar for next Wednesday at 4:00 PM to discuss this further.

Best regards,

Armando Alas
