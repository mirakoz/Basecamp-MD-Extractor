Hi Mike,  
  

Please find our monthly report for August below:

[https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/10dlnyUMbYx2kknGOByHxr\_wlIKsTm7c0uss…](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/10dlnyUMbYx2kknGOByHxr_wlIKsTm7c0ussP1xsM2P0/edit#slide=id.g13b34c7b42a_0_0 "https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/10dlnyUMbYx2kknGOByHxr_wlIKsTm7c0ussP1xsM2P0/edit#slide=id.g13b34c7b42a_0_0")  
  
The results for Google Ads are significantly better compared to July. Cost per lead decreased by 16% while ad spend increased by 14%. The campaigns are being optimized very well. At the campaign level, search\_friendly\_home is performing the best and generating the most conversions. In September, we will focus on improving the performance of underperforming campaigns such as search\_lovejoy.  
  
For Meta Ads, the results are similar to July. To improve Meta Ads performance, we will seek new audiences, as the current audiences have an ad frequency of over 5.  
  
Lastly, we are still waiting for the Meta remarketing audiences to grow. We will update you once they are ready.  
  

If you'd like to schedule a new meeting date, please let us know a suitable time.

Additionally, if you need anything from us, such as additional reports or data before the client meeting, please don't hesitate to ask.  
  
Also, I would like to introduce ourselves: Yeliz and I have taken over the account from Buse, and we'll continue running them with Yigit. We look forward to meeting you at the next meeting.  
  
Best regards,  
Can
