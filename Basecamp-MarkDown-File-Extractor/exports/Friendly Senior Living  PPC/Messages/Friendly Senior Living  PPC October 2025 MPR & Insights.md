**Here are some key results from October.**Overall Spend: $8,325  
Overall CPC: $2.73  
Overall CTR: 3,42%  
Total Conversions:  243 conversions at a CPA of $34.26.  
  
**Monthly Insights & Actions:**

* Compared to last year, cost per click decreased from $3.12 to $2.73.
* Primary lead rate increased from 8.06% to 8.13% compared to the previous month.
* Friendly Home campaign had the highest conversion rate with 10.31%.

**What's Completed in the Previous Month?**

* We updated the Lily Brook video on October 23.
* We paused all video ads except Lily Brook on Meta.
* We had 39 changes on the Google Ads account.
* We added 39 negative keywords.
