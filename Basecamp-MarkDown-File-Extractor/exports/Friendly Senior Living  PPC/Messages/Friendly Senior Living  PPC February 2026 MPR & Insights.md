**Here are some key results from February.**Overall Spend: $8,137

Overall CPC: $2.68

Overall CTR: 3.25%

Total Conversions: 260 conversions at a CPA of $31.30  
  
**Monthly Insights & Actions:**

* The overall conversion rate decreased from 7.56% to 8.58%.
* The overall cost per lead decreased from $32.11 to $31.30 compared to the previous month.
* In Google Ads, the CTR increased by 6% compared to the previous month. The conversion rate increased from 7.27% to 7.71%.
* In Meta Ads, the cost per lead decreased by 4% compared to the previous month. The conversion rate increased from 7.81% to 9.39%.
* The majority of the leads came from the Friendly Home campaign.
* The highest lead rate belonged to the Glenmere campaign with 14.52%.

  
**What's Completed in the Previous Month?**

* We had 32 changes on the Google Ads account.
* We added 53 negative keywords.
