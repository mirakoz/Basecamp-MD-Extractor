**What are the main insights for the month?**

* In Google Ads, we completed the month with a total of 223 conversions, comprised of 33 form submissions, 123 calls from ads, 9 views of the floor plan, and 58 brochure views.

* The conversion rate saw an increase of 6.5% compared to the previous month. However, the cost per conversion increased by 4% in January.

* Throughout the month, we generated a total of 156 primary conversions across all campaigns, with the top-performing campaign, "search\_friendly\_home," achieving 74 conversions.

* Additionally, the impression share for generic campaigns increased by 17% in January.
* In Meta Ads, we received 80 floor plan views, 10 form submissions, 7 phone calls, 60 brochure views, and 3 requests for directions in January.
* The click-through rate (CTR) increased by 77.9% compared to the previous month, and the cost per lead decreased from $13.63 to $9.36 during January.

  
**What did we do during the month?**

* Ad performance was monitored and continuous adjustments were made based on data and insights to continually improve campaign performance.

* Negative keywords have been added based on search term results.

* Meta Ads video campaigns were launched.

**What's next?**  

* Callrail Integration (Waiting)

* Meta remarketing Audiences (Waiting for filling)

* Awaiting turn on Call Tracking.

All the best,  
Yeliz
