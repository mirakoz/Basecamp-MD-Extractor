### Monthly Report & Insights - August 2025

Posted by Yeliz Kolasinli on Sep 5, 2025

[![Yeliz Kolasinli](https://asset1.basecamp.com/2325709/people/18747394/photo/avatar.96.gif "Yeliz Kolasinli")](/2325709/people/18747394)

Hi Mike,  
  
Please find the monthly performance report for August 2025 below.  
  
[https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1HyOSAJONy78Xns6sESaMC2hqJekyrsCrcnf…](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1HyOSAJONy78Xns6sESaMC2hqJekyrsCrcnfrvYUy0TI/edit?slide=id.g127c65e30f2_0_0#slide=id.g127c65e30f2_0_0 "https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1HyOSAJONy78Xns6sESaMC2hqJekyrsCrcnfrvYUy0TI/edit?slide=id.g127c65e30f2_0_0#slide=id.g127c65e30f2_0_0")  
  
**What are the main insights for the month?**  

* Google Ads account reported 81 calls from ads, 22 lead form submissions, 111 floor plan views, 24 brochure views, and 27 phone number clicks.
* At the campaign level, the best-performing campaign was the Cloverwood campaign, which had the highest conversion rate with 10.14%. Lily Brook video ad had the lowest cost per lead with $51.83. We allocated more budget to Lily Brook in August compared to the previous month.
* Although the primary lead rate decreased, all lead rate improved from 15.49% to 16.23%.
* In Meta Ads, we received 134 views of floor plans, 14 form submissions, 8 phone calls, and 20 brochure views.
* We observed increases in the number of floor plan views on both Meta and Google.
* At the campaign level, the best-performing campaign was the Traffic New User Video Campaign with 131 conversions. The majority of conversions came from the Lily Brook video ad, contributing 99 leads.

**What did we do during the month?**

* Adjusted bids and budgets to better align with performance trends and maximize return.
* Added new negative keywords based on search term analysis to improve targeting efficiency and reduce irrelevant traffic.

  

**Recommendations**

* New videos and images are required to refresh ad creatives and maintain engagement for Meta Ads.

**Please let us know if you’d like to discuss the report, we can schedule a meeting.**Best,  
Yeliz
