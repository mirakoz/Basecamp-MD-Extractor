### March 2026 MPR & Insights

Posted by Mirak Ozoglu on Apr 7

[![Mirak Ozoglu](https://asset1.basecamp.com/2325709/people/19154717/photo/avatar.96.gif "Mirak Ozoglu")](/2325709/people/19154717)

Hi Mike,

I hope you're doing well. Here with the URL please find, March 2026 PPC Performance Report for Friendly Senior Living: [https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1BsAQwz8ptjgKW-KSonv-QCHr5O4ftOZ3keU…](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1BsAQwz8ptjgKW-KSonv-QCHr5O4ftOZ3keU809AKXAo/edit?usp=sharing "https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1BsAQwz8ptjgKW-KSonv-QCHr5O4ftOZ3keU809AKXAo/edit?usp=sharing")

Here are some key highlights from March:

**Overall Performance**

* Overall Spend: $7,493
* Overall CPC: $2.91
* Overall CTR: 3.12%
* Total Conversions: 230
* CPA: $32.58

**Monthly Insights & Actions:**

* The overall conversion rate improved from 8.58% to 8.92% compared to the previous month.
* The overall cost per lead increased slightly from $31.30 to $32.58.
* In Google Ads, CTR remained stable at 7.98%. The conversion rate came in at 7.24%. The Friendly Home campaign continued to be the top lead-generating campaign, accounting for 60 primary leads.
* In Google Ads, the Linden Knoll campaign achieved the highest conversion rate at 10.64%.
* In Meta Ads, the Video Campaign delivered the best cost per lead at $9.82, generating 75 leads. The Image Campaign followed with a strong conversion rate of 12.78%.
* Paid advertisements accounted for 25.74% of total website traffic in March.

**What's Completed This Month?**

* We reallocated the budget across campaigns to align with performance trends.
* We improved ad descriptions and headlines across active campaigns.
* We adjusted CPA targets to optimize campaign delivery.

Mirak
