Hi Mike,

Your December PPC Performance Report for Friendly Senior Living is now ready!  
  
[https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/14j7WlRaeE9yKcJQRgmEGCzum1P2fy83ngWz…](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/14j7WlRaeE9yKcJQRgmEGCzum1P2fy83ngWzBQMD-O5A/edit?slide=id.g127c65e30f2_0_0#slide=id.g127c65e30f2_0_0 "https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/14j7WlRaeE9yKcJQRgmEGCzum1P2fy83ngWzBQMD-O5A/edit?slide=id.g127c65e30f2_0_0#slide=id.g127c65e30f2_0_0")  
  
**Here are some key results from December.**Overall Spend: $7,411

Overall CPC: $2.75

Overall CTR: 3.46%

Total Conversions: 232 conversions at a CPA of $31.95  
  
**Monthly Insights & Actions:**

* The cost per lead decreased from $40.03 to $31.95.
* The conversion rate increased by 22% compared to the previous month.
* In Google Ads, the conversion rate increased from 7.17% to 7.50%.
* In Meta Ads, the conversion rate increased to 9.58%, marking the highest level achieved in the past five months and indicating a strong improvement in campaign performance.
* Cloverwood campaign had the highest conversion rate with 10.70%.

**What's Completed in the Previous Month?**

* We had 38 changes on the Google Ads account.
* We added 45 negative keywords.

  
Best regards,

Yeliz
