Our Google Ads campaign generated 173 primary conversions in September, with the best-performing campaign being search\_friendly\_home.

For the Meta Ads, we achieved 184 conversions in September. The top-performing creative was the 175th Anniversary creative, which had a 1.49% click-through rate (CTR) and 182 conversions.

The most cost-effective creative was Cloverwood Creative, with a cost per click (CPC) of $0.48.

Overall click-through rate (CTR) increased by 31% compared to the previous month.

The campaigns on Meta were focused on clicks and we changed the campaign goals to conversions. Moving forward, we'll be optimising towards conversions.
