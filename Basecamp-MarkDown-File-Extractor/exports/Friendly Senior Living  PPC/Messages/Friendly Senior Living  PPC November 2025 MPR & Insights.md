Your November PPC Performance Report for Friendly Senior Living is now ready!  
  
[https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/17iriH1jksRZb9nJqpDpr-Z-8gatNWg0BPUD…](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/17iriH1jksRZb9nJqpDpr-Z-8gatNWg0BPUDzkeIUQ18/edit?slide=id.g127c65e30f2_0_0#slide=id.g127c65e30f2_0_0 "https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/17iriH1jksRZb9nJqpDpr-Z-8gatNWg0BPUDzkeIUQ18/edit?slide=id.g127c65e30f2_0_0#slide=id.g127c65e30f2_0_0")  
  
**Here are some key results from November.**Overall Spend: $8,125  
Overall CPC: $2.82  
Overall CTR: 3.93%  
Total Conversions: 203 conversions at a CPA of $40.03  
  

**Monthly Insights & Actions:**

Overall click-through rate(CTR) increased 14% compared to the previous month.  
  
For Google Ads,  
- The cost per click (CPC) decreased from $5.05 to $4.49.  

- The click through rate(CTR) increased from 7.68% to 7.89%.

The Cloverwood campaign had the highest conversion rate with 10.36%.

In October, we paused the Linden Knoll, Cloverwood, and Friendly Home ads in the video campaign and allocated the entire campaign budget to the Lily Brook ad. Pausing the ads for the 3 locations resulted in a drop in performance.

**What's Completed in the Previous Month?**

* We had 18 changes on the Google Ads account.
* We added 14 negative keywords.
