**What are the main insights for the month?**

Campaigns generated 32 phone calls and 8 form submissions.

The top-performing campaign was the private investigator, which generated 29 conversions with the highest conversion rate of 17.17%.

**Key Performance Metrics;**

* Leads increased significantly from 24 to 40 (+66.7%). This is a strong indicator that the campaign became more effective at converting clicks into leads.
* Cost per Lead improved from $57.83 to $34.14. That’s a 41% decrease, meaning we're acquiring more leads at a much lower cost.
* Conversion Rate increased from 9.64% to 14.13%. This +46.6% gain shows the traffic coming through was more qualified.

**What did we do during the month?**

* We reactivated the Performance Max campaign in July.
* Implemented bid and budget adjustments.
* Added new negative keywords based on search terms.

  

**Recommendations**

Lead quality feedback is very important for campaign optimization.
