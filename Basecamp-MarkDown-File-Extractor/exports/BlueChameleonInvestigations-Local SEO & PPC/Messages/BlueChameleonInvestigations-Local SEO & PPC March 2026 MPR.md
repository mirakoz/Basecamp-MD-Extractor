### March 2026 MPR - PPC

Posted by Mirak Ozoglu on Apr 10

[![Mirak Ozoglu](https://asset1.basecamp.com/2325709/people/19154717/photo/avatar.96.gif "Mirak Ozoglu")](/2325709/people/19154717)

Hi Luciana,

Following up on Burak's email, I wanted to officially introduce myself here on Basecamp! We are thrilled to be taking over the Performance Marketing management for Blue Chameleon Investigations.

You can find the link to our monthly report for March 2026 below:
[https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/12BF9FdU3okmkC4wWt-vj34Sv3TRhM5lV-RF…](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/12BF9FdU3okmkC4wWt-vj34Sv3TRhM5lV-RFa47szxFM/edit?usp=sharing "https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/12BF9FdU3okmkC4wWt-vj34Sv3TRhM5lV-RFa47szxFM/edit?usp=sharing")

**What are the main insights for the month?**

* Google Ads campaigns generated a total of 35 leads in March, maintaining a stable pipeline despite a leaner impression footprint.
* The top-performing campaign was Search - Private Investigator 2.0, which drove 17 conversions at a 16.35% conversion rate, confirming it as the primary growth engine this period.
* Performance Max - Leads delivered the strongest Cost/Conv. at $33.71 among non-brand campaigns, making it a cost-efficient channel worth continued investment.
* Phone call volume surged 90.87% MoM, with 27 calls exceeding 60 seconds recorded in March, signaling strong direct intent from high-quality prospects.

**Key Performance Metrics:**

* Cost/Lead came in at $43.42, reflecting a more targeted traffic mix as impressions were filtered down to higher-intent audiences.
* CTR improved to 5.02%, up from 2.63% in February, demonstrating that ad relevance and audience alignment are trending in the right direction.

**What will we do next month?**

* Add "real estate fraud investigator" and "hire a private investigator" as exact match keywords based on strong conversion signals from March search term data.
* Adjust CPA targets, refresh negative keyword lists, and apply device and location-specific bid adjustments to sharpen campaign efficiency.

To ensure a seamless transition and to review this data in detail, we would love to schedule our Monthly Performance Report (MPR) meeting. Do you have any availability next week to connect? Please let us know what days and times work best for your schedule.

We look forward to speaking with you soon!
