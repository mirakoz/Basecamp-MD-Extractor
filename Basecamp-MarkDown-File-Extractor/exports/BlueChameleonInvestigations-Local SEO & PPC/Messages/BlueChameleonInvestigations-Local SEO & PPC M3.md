**What are the main insights for the month?**

Campaigns generated 18 phone calls and 6 form submissions.

At the beginning of the month, we experienced some issues, possibly due to spam clicks. From June 1 to 11, we generated only 3 leads, but from June 12 to 30, the number of leads increased substantially to 21.

During June 1 to 11, the cost per conversion was $178.09, which then dropped significantly to $40.65 in June 12 - 30.

At the campaign level, the Private Investigator campaign delivered the most effective performance, generating 17 phone calls and 6 form submissions.

**What did we do during the month?**

Implemented bid and budget adjustments.

Added new negative keywords based on search terms.

Implemented ad scheduling.

**Recommendations**  
Lead quality feedback is very important for campaign optimization.

**Can Basaloglu**   

Hi Luciana,

Even if you haven’t seen any ads before, clicking a link with UTM parameters will still store those parameters. So even clicking the URL I shared above can cause it to keep appearing for you.

Apparently, this was caused by a setting in the HandL UTM Grabber plugin: *“Append UTM variables to all links automatically.”* We can deactivate it, as it doesn’t serve any purpose for us at the moment.

Initially, I thought the parameters were embedded in the "Get in Touch" buttons, but that’s not the case. Also, this doesn’t affect Google Ads tracking, and everything will display normally for other users.

You can check the attached screenshots for reference.

Best,

Can
