Keyword Research

Investigation into dozens of potential terms for optimization based on competitor keywords, the suggested keywords, Google's recommendations, and our recommendations.

Includes details on search volumes and competition levels.

Keyword Recommendations

Based on our early knowledge of the website's audience, targeting, and key terms, we've made recommendations for our starting optimizations in the "Recommended KWs" tab in the report.

Note that these will need to be approved by you prior to the proceeding; we won't move forward until you've given us the go-ahead. You need to select [10] Primary Keywords.

Client's Current Keyword Rankings

Using external tools we've compiled an exhaustive list of the website's current ranking keywords, their positions, and their ranking trends.  This helps to inform our research and recommendations.

Competitor Existing Keyword Rankings

Using external tools we've compiled an exhaustive list of each of the competitor's current ranking keywords, their positions, and their ranking trends.  This helps to inform our research and recommendations, as well as areas of opportunity for further competitive growth.

Competitor Keyword Research

An index of all of the current frequently used terms and phrases.  While the competitors may not be ranking for these terms, it's recognized that these terms are used in frequency across the competitors' sites and may be targeted terms.  This too helps inform our research and recommendations.

Competitor Backlinks

An Exhaustive index of the existing backlinks for each top competitor.  This allows us to identify any opportunities to appear in the same off-site spaces that the competitors do while expanding our authority generation.

Competitor Site Assessment

A purely technical review of the client's site's - number of indexed pages, the status of optimizations, current search engine links, and the status of social channels.  This gives us a better idea of where the client currently stands against their top competition.

Client Site Assessment

By comparison, a technical review of the website's current status of indexed pages, optimizations, search engine links, and social channels is necessary.  This includes the website's backlinking profile as well, to be compared against the depth of each competitor's relative efforts.
