**Can Basaloglu**   

We can't directly flag the account for our services, as only certain industries, such as medical service providers, have that option. In our case, we can request a manual review from Google Support, which we have already done and are currently awaiting their response.

Additionally, we’ve noticed that website access is restricted in certain locations. Google uses crawlers to verify if ads comply with its policies, and it seems they are encountering difficulties processing the site. This is likely why some ads are being disapproved under the "Destination Not Accessible" policy, which may also explain why our appeals have been unsuccessful.

We are already excluding irrelevant locations in ads both automatically and manually, so there is no need for additional access limitations. However, if the client has other concerns regarding website access from certain locations, we can explore alternative solutions.

Attached are screenshots of the website limitations and Google Ads policy regarding "Destination Not Accessible."
