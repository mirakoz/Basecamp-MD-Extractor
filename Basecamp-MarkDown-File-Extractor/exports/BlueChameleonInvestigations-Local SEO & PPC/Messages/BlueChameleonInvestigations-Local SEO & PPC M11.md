**Monthly Insights & Actions:**  
  

* Despite lower click volume, lead performance remained stable at 38 total leads, matching January’s results. This stability was driven by a strong improvement in conversion rate, which increased from 13.01% to 15.97% (+22.7%). This indicates that although we brought in fewer visitors, the traffic quality improved and converted at a higher rate.
* Phone calls slightly decreased (-3.6%), while lead forms increased by 10%, showing a small shift in conversion behavior rather than overall demand loss.
* Cost per Lead remained essentially flat ($38.40 → $38.46), meaning efficiency was maintained.
