We have a minor request regarding the cookie consent banner on the website.  
  
Currently, on desktop, the cookie banner appears at the bottom of the page, making it easy to miss. This is not an issue on mobile, as the banner covers a larger portion of the screen. If users do not consent to cookies, we will be unable to track any activity on the website.  
  
To ensure users don't miss the banner, we recommend setting it as a pop-up or placing it at the top of the page. Users will still have the option to decline cookies, but this adjustment will help improve visibility and ensure proper tracking.  
  

Attached are screenshots of the desktop and mobile pages, as well as an example of our suggested pop-up placement.

Please let me know if you need any further clarification.
