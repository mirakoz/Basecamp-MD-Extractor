- We started managing the Google Ads account in March 12th and updated performance max campaign. After campaign structure and ad copy approval, we activated search campaigns on March 17th.

- In March between 12-31, account spent $798.10 and generated 23 conversions, including 6 lead form submissions and 17 phone calls.

- Cost per conversion was $34.70

- Performance Max campaign generated 5 phone calls and 1 lead forms, and it was the cheapest campaign in terms of CPA after Brand campaign.

During the month, we;

-Created and activated new Google Ads Search campaigns.

-Created new conversion actions for lead forms and phone calls and applied the tracking using Google Tag Manager.

-Audience segments were added under observation for a better understanding of the converting audience.

-Created sitelinks, callouts structured snippets and call extension and added them to active campaigns.

-Examined search terms to target the most relevant ones and eliminate those with low performance.

-Made changes to improve our optimization score, helping maintain a consistent ad rank.

-Adjusted budget and bidding strategies to optimize results and make the most of our ad spend.
