**What are the main insights for the month?**

Campaigns generated 32 phone calls and 11 form submissions.

The top-performing campaign was the Performance Max campaign, which generated 16 conversions. Search - Brand is the most cost-efficient campaign with $8.53 cost per lead. Search - AI Max campaign had the highest conversion rate with 29.17%.

**Key Performance Changes;**

* The number of lead form submissions increased from 8 to 11.
* Avg. CPC increased by 14.22% due to the higher Avg. CPC of the new Search – AI Max campaign.
* Click-through rate(CTR) increased from 3.10% to 3.15%.

**What did we do during the month?**

* The Background Check campaign has been paused, and the Search - AI Max campaign has been opened instead.
* Added new negative keywords based on search terms.

**Recommendations**

Lead quality feedback is very important for campaign optimization.
