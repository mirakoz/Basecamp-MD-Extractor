**Traffic drop reason:**

This was just approved and will be implemented - **Missing all service pages from the top navigation**: Search engines prioritize easily accessible content, and if key service pages are buried deep within the site, they may not be crawled or indexed efficiently. Poor navigation structure also leads to a bad user experience, increasing bounce rates and reducing conversions. Users may struggle to find the services offered, leading to missed business opportunities. Additionally, internal linking strength is weakened, which can lower the authority and ranking potential of those service pages. Clear, top-level navigation ensures visibility, both for users and search engines.

Apart from that, we’ve identified a drop in rankings over the past month for the queries “private investigator Monroe NC,” “private investigator Charlotte,” “private investigators near me,” “private investigator near me,” and “Charlotte private investigator,” which has contributed to decreased clicks and overall traffic.

After reviewing the Search Console, we observed that the top queries received **fewer clicks** as compared to the previous month –

* private investigator monroe nc (0-3=-3)
* private investigator charlotte (0-2=-2)
* private investigators near me (0-2=-2)
* private investigator near me (1-2=-1)
* charlotte private investigator (0-1=-1)
