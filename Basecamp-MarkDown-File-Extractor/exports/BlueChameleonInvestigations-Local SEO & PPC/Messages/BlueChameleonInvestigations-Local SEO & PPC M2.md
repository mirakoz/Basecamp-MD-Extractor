**What are the main insights for the month?**

In April, our Google Ads campaigns generated a total of 23 phone calls and 10 form submissions, resulting in an overall conversion rate of 10.12% and a cost per conversion of $39.72. It's important to note that the transition to the WordPress website impacted conversion tracking during the first two weeks of the month.  
  
At the campaign level, the Private Investigator campaign delivered the most effective performance, generating 15 phone calls and 6 form submissions. It achieved a conversion rate of 12.28% and a click-through rate of 7.38%.

On the keyword level, the keyword that provides the most conversions is "background check services.", which generated 6 conversions in April.

**What did we do during the month?**

* Set up new conversion tracking to support the WordPress website transition.

* Created a new ad group under the Private Investigation campaign.

* Added new negative keywords based on search terms.

Implemented bid and budget adjustments.

**Recommendations**

* Lead quality feedback is very important for campaign optimization.
* Adjustments to capture UTM data in the lead form submissions to identify the source, medium, and campaign of each lead.

Also, I would like to introduce myself; I have taken over the account from Mücahit, and we'll continue running it with Can. Look forward to meeting you at the meeting.  
  
Best regards,  
Yeliz

Hi Luciana,  
  

I just checked the leads through the backend access, and it looks like the Performance Max campaign is attracting spam leads. This is something we run into time to time, especially with lead based conversions. Since these are easier to complete, Google tends to optimize toward cheaper conversions, even if they’re low quality (or spam/bot in our case). Performance Max also struggles in these cases because it can’t distinguish between qualified and unqualified leads on its own.

For now, I’ll pause the Performance Max campaign. Once we have UTM parameters (with Google Click IDs) for contact forms set up, we can upload offline conversions and use that data to optimize the campaign based on actual lead quality.
