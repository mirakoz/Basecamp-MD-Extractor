Hi Luciana,

For PPC, yes, it's possible to do both but, if the client wants to advertise nationwide, I recommend increasing the budget. This way, the performance of the existing campaigns won't be negatively affected. Otherwise, the budget per campaign will remain quite low. Especially if a nationwide campaign is planned, a significantly higher budget will be required. Additionally, due to the limitations of our IO, we can currently only advertise locally or regionally.  
  

If the client is considering increasing the budget, I can inform the Growth Team to contact you.  
  
Best,  
Can
