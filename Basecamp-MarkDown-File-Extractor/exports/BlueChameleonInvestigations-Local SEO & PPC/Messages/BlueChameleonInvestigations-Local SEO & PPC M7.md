**Monthly Insights & Actions:**

* Avg. CPC decreased by 4.64% compared to the previous month.
* The conversion rate decreased from 17.86% to 14.29%.
* The click through rate (CTR) has reached its highest level in the last 4 months, at 6.74%.
* The budget of the AI Max campaign was reduced this month due to its underperformance. (Spent $256.33 in September)
* The top-performing campaign was the Private Investigator campaign, which generated 18 conversions.
* The highest conversion rate belonged to the Brand campaign.
* The Pmax - Lead campaign’s conversion rate increased from 13.46% to 16.34%.
