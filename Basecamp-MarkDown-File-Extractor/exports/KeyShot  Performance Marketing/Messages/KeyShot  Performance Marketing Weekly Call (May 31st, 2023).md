**Bailey Liu**   
Thanks for the notes, Fathy and Burak.   
  
Feedback regarding June's budget:   
  
**For Google**   

* Please prepare forecasts based on 1) a 50% budget increase, and 2) a budget 100% increase
* As discussed in the call, in the same forecast, please prepare estimated ROAS based on 1) GA4 (web) purchase value, and 2) Purchase value
* It's good to see that we are able to increase the revenue while keeping the floor for ROAS. However, my only concern is that we are cannibalizing our organic traffic & revenue for the paid search as the majority of the results in paid search channels come from brand keywords. I do see our organic revenue decreased in May. (Combining paid search and organic search we are still growing, but we do see the organic decreases since we increase the budget for ppc.)
* Following the point above, it means we really need to improve the non-branded keyword campaigns and start to drive revenue from there. Let's put this as the top priority in June.

  
**For Paid Social**

* Let's go with the budget we agreed on here [https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1UQFcGKLFJjFrxefrRGCA25zh07ZFJ-0SreE…](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1UQFcGKLFJjFrxefrRGCA25zh07ZFJ-0SreEO-iGDr8E/edit#gid=1052882910 "https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1UQFcGKLFJjFrxefrRGCA25zh07ZFJ-0SreEO-iGDr8E/edit#gid=1052882910")

  
Let me know if any questions. Thanks.   
  
Bailey.
