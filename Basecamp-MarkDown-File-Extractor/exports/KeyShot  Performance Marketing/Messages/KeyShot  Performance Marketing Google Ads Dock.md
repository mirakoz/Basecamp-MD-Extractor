### Google Ads Dock

Posted by Asger Teglgaard on Mar 21, 2025

[![Asger Teglgaard](https://asset1.basecamp.com/2325709/people/18906982/photo/avatar.96.gif "Asger Teglgaard")](/2325709/people/18906982)

Hi

We're expanding our KeyShot Dock Google Ads campaigns to include additional regions and new ad groups focused on our 3D asset management capabilities. Please implement these changes as outlined below:

**See the ad copy and landing page URLs in this sheet:***[https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/109eIf0X4diJQgDYKNPQsoxa05QcB2CkU8it…](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/109eIf0X4diJQgDYKNPQsoxa05QcB2CkU8itH8n9N1bI/edit?usp=sharing "https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/109eIf0X4diJQgDYKNPQsoxa05QcB2CkU8itH8n9N1bI/edit?usp=sharing")*  
Tabs:  
- Ads Planner - Beyond Basic DAM  
- Ads Planner - 3D assets  
- Ads Planner - Always On

**Budget Allocation**

We have a total Google Ads budget of approximately $10,000 USD for April. Please allocate this budget strategically across our campaigns with the following considerations:

* **Priority 1:** The new 3D Asset Management Campaign should receive sufficient budget to gain traction (suggest 25-30%)
* **Priority 2:** Competitor campaigns consistently deliver quality leads and should maintain strong visibility
* **Priority 3:** Regional expansion markets should receive proportional budgets based on market size and potential
* **Priority 4:** Workflow Campaign needs sufficient testing budget across all three ad groups

Please provide a recommended budget breakdown by campaign and region based on historical performance data and potential return. We're reducing our LinkedIn spend to accommodate this expanded Google Ads investment.

  
**Regional Expansion**

**Current Coverage:**

* Competitor Campaigns: Denmark, UK, US
* Product Generic Campaigns: UK, US

**Expanded Coverage (for all campaigns):**

* Denmark
* Sweden
* Norway
* Finland
* United Kingdom
* Ireland
* Belgium
* Luxembourg
* Netherlands
* United States

  
**Workflow Campaign**

**Ad Groups:**

* Workflow Automation
* Brand Consistency
* Integration & Efficiency

  
**DAM Competitors Campaign**

**Ad Group:**

* DAM Competitors (targeting competitor terms)

  
**Product Generic Campaign**

**Ad Group:**

* Digital Asset Management Tool

  
**New: 3D Asset Management Campaign**

**Ad Groups:**

* Primary 3D DAM Keywords
* Industry/Use-Case Specific
* 3D Asset Workflow & Sharing

  
**Display** **Campaigns**

We're also launching two new display campaigns:

1. Competitor Display Campaign (replacing the Qbank competitor campaign)
2. Workflow Display Campaign

Our design team is currently preparing the visual assets for these campaigns. We'll provide the creatives once they're finalized. Set deadline for next week.

**3D Asset Management Campaign Details**This new campaign highlights our recently released 3D capabilities, including GLB file preview and sharing. We've structured it into three targeted ad groups with specific keywords and messaging for each.  
  
  

Please use the ad copies, landing pages and keywords we've provided for each ad group. **The keywords are suggestions, so please make some research and figure out if there is any volume and so on.** Come up with potential additional keywords. 

Let me know if you need any additional information to implement these changes. We'd like to launch these expansions by Wednesday 26th. I know the display ads might come a bit later because we have to wait for the visuals.
