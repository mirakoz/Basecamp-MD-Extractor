Thanks for sharing it, Fathy. The forecast looks fine with me overall.   
  
A few thoughts after reviewing the forecast:   
  

* The first week of June is a lot lower than what we forecast here (3.8 ROAS actual vs. 5.2 ROAS forecasted.) Given that we usually see a strong performance at the beginning of the month, I'd track the campaign performance very closely from a weekly pacing standpoint.
* I see most of the campaigns are set as "Max conversion" bidding strategy, should we switch to "Max conversion value" so we can push the ROAS?
* We spend a lot on the non-branded campaigns, but they haven't been delivering results as expected. These campaigns are must-win battles for us because those keywords are our core product & solution, let's have a list of recommendation on how we can improve those campaign and review them next week!

  
Let me know your thoughts. Thanks!
