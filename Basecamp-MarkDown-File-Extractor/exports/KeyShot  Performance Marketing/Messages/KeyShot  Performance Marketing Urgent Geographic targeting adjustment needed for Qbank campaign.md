Hi ALM team,

Our Qbank InMail campaign (all of the Qbank campaigns) targeting needs immediate adjustment. While generating 300+ leads, it's currently reaching the entire EMEA region instead of just our Nordic focus (Denmark, Finland, Sweden, Norway). This means we're spending budget on leads outside our target market.

**Action items needed:**

* Update targeting to Nordic countries only

Given our previous discussions focused specifically on Nordic targeting, I want to ensure we're aligned moving forward. Please advise when we can implement these changes.
