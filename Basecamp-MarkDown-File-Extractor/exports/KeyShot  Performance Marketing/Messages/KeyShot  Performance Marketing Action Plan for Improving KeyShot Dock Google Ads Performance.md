### Action Plan for Improving KeyShot Dock Google Ads Performance

Posted by Asger Teglgaard on Feb 7, 2025

[![Asger Teglgaard](https://asset1.basecamp.com/2325709/people/18906982/photo/avatar.96.gif "Asger Teglgaard")](/2325709/people/18906982)

Thanks for the productive discussion about our Google Ads performance for KeyShot Dock. I wanted to summarize the key points we discussed and outline next steps.

Regarding the lack of Google Ads conversions, we identified several testing opportunities that I'd like to explore:

**Landing Page Testing**

* Let's proceed with the A/B testing between our current KeyShot Dock product page and the direct demo request page. As we discussed, it makes sense to do a 50/50 split to see which drives better conversion performance.
* Maybe we should consider testing <https://www.keyshot.com/digital-asset-management/> too

**User Behavior Analysis**

* I've spoken with Josh about implementing heat mapping on our key pages. He's looking into what tools we currently have available or could implement. Once he confirms the technical details, I'll connect him with you to get this set up.
* We should track user behavior across the main KeyShot Dock page, demo request page, and product walkthrough page to better understand where users might be dropping off.

**Campaign Optimization**

* Your suggestion to analyze impression share data by specific campaigns and regions makes sense. I'd be particularly interested in seeing the breakdown for US campaigns vs our Danish competitor campaigns that have converted previously.

Let me know what you need from our side to move forward with these tests. I'm particularly keen to get the landing page testing underway as soon as possible.

Happy to jump on a call to discuss any of these points in more detail.
