**Creative Assets**

- Use existing ad creative from EMEA campaigns

- No changes needed to ad copy or visuals

**Campaign Settings**

- Keep all other settings (bidding strategy, optimization goals, etc.) identical to EMEA campaigns

- Maintain same frequency caps and dayparting

- Use same conversion tracking setup
