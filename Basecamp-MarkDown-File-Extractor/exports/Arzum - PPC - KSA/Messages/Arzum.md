**Can Basaloglu**   

Here is the budget distribution plan for October and November. A detailed forecast wouldn’t be meaningful at this stage, as we’re testing new audience targeting options and expecting improved results compared to the previous setup. For example, the Okka campaign's cost per result has been cut in half, our reach among female audiences has increased, and ROAS has risen significantly. We can apply a similar strategy for the awareness campaigns, though in a less aggressive manner than the sales campaign.

  
In October, we can run separate awareness campaigns for different product groups, as discussed in the meeting. In November, our main focus will be on 11.11 and White Friday.

The Okka campaign performance looks promising after the recent changes. For other products (such as kitchen appliances and espresso machines), performance is still below expectations, but it’s too early to draw conclusions. We’ve distributed the budget based on our initial outcomes.

Also, here are the creative specifications for Meta Ads:

Image:   
Aspect Ratio: (1.91:1) - 1200\*628  
Aspect Ratio: (9:16)  - 1080\*1920  
Aspect Ratio: (1:1)  - 1080\*1080  
Max File Size: 30 MB  
File Type: JPEG or PNG  
  
Video:  
Aspect Ratio: (1.91:1) - 1200\*628 (Horizontal)  
Aspect Ratio: (1:1) - 1080\*1080 (Feed size)  
Aspect Ratio: (4:5) - 1440\*1800 (Optional/Feed Size)  
Aspect Ratio: (9:16) - 1080\*1920 (Story Size)  
  
Maximum File Size: 4 GB  
File Type: MP4 or MOV  
  
It is recommended that three different aspect ratios be created for each image and video.  
  
Let me us know if you have any questions.  
  
Best,
