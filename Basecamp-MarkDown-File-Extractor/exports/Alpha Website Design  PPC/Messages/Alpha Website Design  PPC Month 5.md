**What are the main insights for the month?**

In July, our campaigns generated a total of 144 conversions, including 1 website call, 4 form submissions, and 139 third-page views, at a highly efficient cost per conversion of $3.43. Notably, we achieved our highest number of form fill conversions to date.  
  
We also recorded our highest monthly impressions, reaching 79,079, indicating strong visibility and reach.  
  
Contributing to improved cost efficiency, the cost per click (CPC) decreased by 12.20% compared to the previous period.  
  
In the Performance Max campaign, we observed search terms such as "pay per click advertising for attorneys" and "digital marketing for dentists," indicating that we’re reaching a highly interested and qualified audience.

**What did we do during the month?**

The Search - Marketing Services campaign's bid strategy changed from manual CPC to Maximize conversions.

We enabled the Search Max feature in our search campaign, which helped expand the campaign’s reach and capture a broader audience.
