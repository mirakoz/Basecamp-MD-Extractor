**Here are some key results from October.**

Overall Spend: $463.05  
CTR: 3.03%

**Monthly Insights & Actions:**

  

* In October, the campaign maintained solid performance in terms of both engagement and conversions. Campaigns generated 33 form submissions, 1 call from the website, and 1 call from ads.

* The Cost per Conversion dropped to $13.23, down from $26.64 in September, showing that the campaign became significantly more cost-efficient in generating leads.

* CTR slightly increased to 3.03% (up from 2.38% in September), suggesting ads are more appealing and generating better interest among users.

* Additionally, we received 10 third-page view conversions from our campaigns.

  

**What's Completed in the Previous Month?**

* We prepared and shared the Digital Resume campaign structure and ad copy.
* We added 16 negative keywords based on search term reports to filter out irrelevant traffic and improve overall ad efficiency.
