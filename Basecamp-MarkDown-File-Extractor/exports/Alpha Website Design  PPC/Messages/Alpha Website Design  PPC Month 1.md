**What are the main insights for the month?**

In March, the campaign delivered solid engagement and visibility. We generated 2 form submissions.  
  
The click-through rate reached 6.33%, indicating strong ad engagement and relevance to the target audience.  
  
The conversion rate was 6.45%, showing that a good portion of users who clicked were highly interested. Additionally, the cost per click (CPC) was $16.11  
  
We maintained a strong impression share, ensuring high visibility in relevant search results.

**What did we do during the month?**  

* We prepared and shared strategic campaign recommendations to support ongoing performance improvements.

  

* Ad performance was monitored, and continuous adjustments were made based on data and insights to continually improve campaign performance.

  

* We added new negative keywords based on search term reports to filter out irrelevant traffic and improve overall ad efficiency.

**What's Next?**  

* We'll create the Performance Max campaign.

* Continuous optimization of search terms will be carried out to improve targeting accuracy and eliminate irrelevant traffic.
