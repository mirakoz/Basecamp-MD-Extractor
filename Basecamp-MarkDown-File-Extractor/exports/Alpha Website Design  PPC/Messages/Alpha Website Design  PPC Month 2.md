**What are the main insights for the month?**In April, the click-through rate(CTR) increased from 6.33% to 7.53%, indicating strong ad engagement and relevance to the target audience.

We maintained a strong impression share, ensuring high visibility in relevant search results. The impression share increased from 42.26% to 43.56%.

**What did we do during the month?**  

* We've prepared and shared a set of ad copy and keywords that can be used to promote SEO and PPC services.

* Ad performance was monitored, and continuous adjustments were made based on data and insights to continually improve campaign performance.

  

* We added new negative keywords based on search term reports to filter out irrelevant traffic and improve overall ad efficiency.

  
  
**What's Next?**  

* We'll launch a new campaign for SEO and PPC services.
