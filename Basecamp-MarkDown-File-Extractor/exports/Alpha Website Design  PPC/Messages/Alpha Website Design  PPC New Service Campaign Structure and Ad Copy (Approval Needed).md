Thanks Can,   
  
  

I reviewed the ad copy you sent for the Digital Resume campaign and overall, it’s a strong foundation. The structure makes sense with separate ad groups for Job Seekers and Professionals/Freelancers, but I’d like to make a few refinements before launch to tighten up performance.

**Here are my notes:**

* The headlines and descriptions read well but feel a bit repetitive across both ad groups. Let’s mix in more emotional and benefit-focused angles.

  + Keep lines like *“Your Career Deserves More”* and *“Elevate Your Career Brand.”*
  + Replace *“Contact Us for Details”* with something like *“Free Consultation Available”* or *“See Live Examples.”*
* The “resume builder”keyword has high search volume but attracts DIY traffic that’s not likely to convert. I suggest excluding it or setting it as a low-priority keyword.
* Let’s end each description with a clearer CTA, such as *“Start your project today”* or *“Book a free consult.”*
* Add one or two headlines that include the brand name *“by Alpha Website Design”* for trust and recognition.
* Include at least one dynamic keyword insertion ad to improve relevance for local searches.
