**Monthly Insights & Actions:**  

* Campaigns run in December generated a total of 88 conversions. Of these conversions, 73 were form submissions, 11 were WhatsApp contacts, and 4 were phone calls.
* The UK campaigns had the highest cost per conversion at ₺3,961.63, but they also achieved the highest conversion rate at 4.51%.
* The highest number of conversions (46) came from the Germany and Austria campaigns.
* In December, Google Ads campaigns recorded 55 form submissions, 11 WhatsApp conversions, and 4 phone call conversions.
* Meta campaigns have been paused since December 8 due to CRM issues.
* All campaigns were paused on December 30.
