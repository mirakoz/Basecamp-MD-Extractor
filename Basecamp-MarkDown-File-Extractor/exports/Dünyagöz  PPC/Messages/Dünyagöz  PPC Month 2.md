**Here are some key results from September.**

Overall Spend: ₺530,694.94

Total Conversions: 366 conversions at a cost per conversion of ₺1,449.99.  

The campaigns conducted in September generated a total of 366 conversions, 342 form submissions, 21 WhatsApp interactions, and 3 phone calls.

**Monthly Insights & Actions:**

* Since July 28, a total of 814 Webform and WhatsApp conversions have been generated, resulting in 19 appointments, corresponding to a conversion-to-appointment rate of 2.33%.
* Although MENA countries have a lower conversion rate, they deliver the lowest cost per conversion at ₺822.24 due to their significantly lower cost per click compared to European countries.
* Between September 1–4, no form submission conversions were recorded in Google campaigns due to a server issue, which negatively affected our conversion rate and cost per conversion.

  
  

**What Has Been Done So Far:**

* Budgets have been restructured based on campaign performance and search volumes.
* Negative keywords were added in Google Ads to reach a more relevant audience.

  

**Plans for October:**  
  

* Budgets will be restructured based on campaign performance and search volumes.
* After reviewing potential lead form conversions, campaign optimizations will be made according to the feedback.
* Negative keyword exclusions in Google Ads will continue to ensure reaching a more relevant audience.
* SMS verification will be tested for Meta Ads instant forms.
* Separate ad sets for Facebook and Instagram will be tested in Meta Ads.
* A campaign test targeting iPhone users will be conducted in Meta Ads.

  

**Meeting notes:**

Lead form and WhatsApp conversion rates will be compared to determine whether to proceed with instant forms or WhatsApp targeting on Meta.
