Hi Ayşegül,

Until today, we’ve spent TRY 108,804.10 on Google Ads, generating 34 conversions, and TRY 102,467.31 on Meta Ads, resulting in 97 conversions. The best-performing Google Ads campaigns have been “Brand - Germany & Austria,” “Eye Color Change - UK,” and “Eyelid Plastic - UK.” The conversion rate has improved compared to last month, with the cost per conversion currently at TRY 3,200.

On Meta Ads, the “Eyelid Plastic Surgery - UK” campaign has delivered the highest conversion rate, while the MENA campaign recorded the highest number of total conversions.

We’ll prepare more detailed insights for our monthly meeting.
