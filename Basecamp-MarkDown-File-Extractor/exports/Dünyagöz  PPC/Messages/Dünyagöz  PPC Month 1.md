**What Has Been Done So Far:**

* On July 28, campaigns were launched on both Google Ads and Meta Ads.
* On July 30, the “Meta Ads – UK – Eye Color Change” campaign was launched.
* On July 31, the “Meta Ads – GER & AUS – Eye Color Change” campaign was launched.
* Meta Ads campaigns were initially launched with a focus on starting WhatsApp chats.
* On August 11, test campaigns focused on form submissions were launched.
* On August 15, the WhatsApp chat–focused campaigns on Meta Ads were stopped, and the ads continued only with campaigns focused on form submissions.

  

**Plans for September:**  
  

* The budgets of the Eye Color Change campaigns will be reallocated to higher-performing campaigns.
* Budgets will be readjusted based on campaign performance and search volumes.
* After reviewing lead form conversions, campaigns will be optimized according to feedback.
* Negative keyword exclusions will continue in Google Ads to reach a more accurate audience.

  

**Meeting notes:**

Lead data will be obtained from the client at regular intervals. The client will compare this data and the lead data we provide, and feedback will be shared.
