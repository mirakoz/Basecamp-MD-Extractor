**Here are some key results from November.**Overall Spend: ₺280,052.94

CTR: 5.73%

Avg. CPC: ₺156.63

Total Conversions: 91 conversions  
Cost Per Conversion: ₺3,077.50

**Monthly Insights & Actions:**  
  

* The UK campaigns had the highest cost per conversion at ₺3,669.84, but this location also had the highest conversion rate at 5.51%.
* The highest number of conversions came from the Germany & Austria campaigns, with a total of 54.
* With the Meta Ads campaigns paused, the MENA campaigns, which continued only on Google Ads, showed significantly low performance in November.
* The Meta Ads campaigns were paused from October 30th until the end of November due to a payment error.
* The conversion rate increased from 4.54% to 5.09%.
* In November, the Google Ads campaigns recorded 64 form submissions, 24 WhatsApp conversions, and 3 phone call conversions.

**What's Completed in the Previous Month?**  

* Budgets were restructured based on campaign performance and search volumes.
* Negative keywords were added in Google Ads to reach a more accurate audience.
* On November 14, new ad groups were created using high-performing search terms that included ‘Turkey’ and ‘Türkei.’

  
**Plans for December:**  
  
Separate ad sets for Facebook and Instagram will be tested in Meta Ads.  
  
**Meeting notes:**  
  
We'll focus on keywords and search terms that include the words ‘Turkey’ and ‘Türkei’.  
  
Please let us know if you have any questions. We're always here to help!  
  

Best regards,
