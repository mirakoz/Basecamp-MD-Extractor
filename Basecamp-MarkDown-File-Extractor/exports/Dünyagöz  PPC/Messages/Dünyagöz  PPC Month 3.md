**Here are some key results from October.**

Overall Spend: ₺501,936.12  
CTR: 0.37%  
Avg. CPC: ₺52.73  
Total Conversions: 383 conversions at a highly efficient cost per conversion of ₺1,310.54.

**Monthly Insights & Actions:**

  

* In October, the campaign maintained solid performance in terms of both engagement and conversions. Campaigns generated 363 form submissions, 18 WhatsApp messages, and 2 calls from ads.

* The Cost per Conversion dropped to ₺1,310.54, down from ₺1,449.99 in September, showing that the campaign became significantly more cost-efficient in generating leads.

* The Conversion Rate increased to 4.02% (up from 3.07% in September), suggesting ads are more appealing and generating better interest among users.
* On October 31st, Meta campaigns were paused due to payment issues.
* Although MENA countries have a lower conversion rate, they deliver the lowest cost per conversion at ₺1,004.48 due to their significantly lower cost per click compared to European countries.

  

**What's Completed in the Previous Month?**

* Budgets were adjusted based on campaign performance and search volumes.
* Negative keyword exclusions were applied in Google Ads to reach a more qualified audience.
* The Brand - MENA campaign was launched on October 28.
* The Eye Color Change - MENA campaign was launched on October 31.

  
**Plans for November:**  

* Budgets will be adjusted based on campaign performance and search volumes.
* Negative keyword exclusions in Google Ads will continue to reach a more qualified audience.
* In Meta Ads, separate ad sets for Facebook and Instagram will be tested.

  

**Meeting notes:**

* If there is sufficient search volume for Turkey in the Trifocal campaign, a separate ad group will be created.
* A competitor price analysis will be conducted and shared.

Please let us know if you have any questions. We're always here to help!  
  

Best regards,
