**Can Basaloglu**   
Hi Ayşegül,  
  
Here are a few issues with the German ad copy (please see the attached screenshot for reference):  
1. The word contains an unnecessary "e". It should be "AUGENLIDOPERATIONEN".

2. The singular article "der" is incorrectly used with the plural word "Operationen." The correct usage is "Bei Augenlidoperationen" (plural) or "Bei der Augenlidoperation" (singular).  
3. "Mit der Anwendung dieser Operation..." is the correct usage rather than "Mit dieser operationen Anwendung..."  
4. The correct preposition is "von" instead of "für."  
  

Regarding the landing pages, the about us pages are fine and can be used. However, the brand landing pages and this one page:

[https://www.dunyagoz.com/tr/landings/augenfarbenanderung-flaak-pro/flaak-pr…](https://www.dunyagoz.com/tr/landings/augenfarbenanderung-flaak-pro/flaak-pro "https://www.dunyagoz.com/tr/landings/augenfarbenanderung-flaak-pro/flaak-pro"),

don’t have a video in the designated video section.

Also, the FAQ sections on all landing pages are currently empty. It would be good to populate them; if that’s not possible, it’s better to remove the FAQ headline altogether.

Best,  
Can
