[https://lookerstudio.google.com/reporting/f78455b6-2b67-4a3f-997b-50f5d9e6c…](https://lookerstudio.google.com/reporting/f78455b6-2b67-4a3f-997b-50f5d9e6c3b5 "https://lookerstudio.google.com/reporting/f78455b6-2b67-4a3f-997b-50f5d9e6c3b5")  
  
In this report, you'll see the results from PPC campaign, our insights, recommendations and completed tasks during the month.  
This report allows you or the client to monitor the results on a daily/weekly/monthly basis. To give you an example, you can simple navigate to "Google Ads - Detailed" page and change the dates from the top-right menu.  
  
**What are the main insights for the month?**

* The campaigns were launched for only 9 days in December. During this period, we spent $137.41, which resulted in 6 phone calls and 1 form submission.

* The conversion rate was 15.22%, and the cost per conversion unit was $19.63.

* The keywords that generated the highest traffic were "car repair memphis tn" and "car repair shops near me," resulting in a total of 7 conversions.

* We will see and evaluate the actual results in January.

  

**What did we do during the month?**

* We set up conversions in the account, created a campaign, and launched it.

* We created negative keyword lists.

  

**What's next?**

* We'll focus on bid strategy optimizations.

* We'll continue with ongoing Search term optimizations.
