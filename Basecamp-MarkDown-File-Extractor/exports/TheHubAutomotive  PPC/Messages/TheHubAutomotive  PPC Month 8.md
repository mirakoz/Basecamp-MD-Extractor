**What are the main insights for the month?**

* In July, we saw strong improvements across key metrics, showing that the campaign is moving in a positive direction:
* Conversions increased by 73.7%, reaching a total of 33, a clear sign that we're attracting more qualified leads.
* The conversion rate also improved significantly from 8.88% to 14.35% (+61.6%), meaning more of the people clicking on ads are taking action.
* We achieved these results at a much more efficient rate, with the cost per conversion dropping by 42.7% to $15.04, our lowest cost per conversion ever.
* Traffic to the site increased, with impressions up 23.6% (3400 to 4201) and clicks up 7.5%. Meanwhile, the average cost per click decreased by 7.4% (from $2.33 to $2.16), improving the overall value of our ad spend.

**What did we do during the month?**

We added new negative keywords based on search term reports to filter out irrelevant traffic and improve overall ad efficiency.

****What's needed?****

Lead quality feedback is very important for campaign optimization. We need feedback from the client regarding qualified leads.
