**What are the main insights for the month?**

In March, our campaign achieved notable results, receiving a total of 11 calls from ads, 8 calls from the website, and 4 form submissions. The conversion rate increased from 9.18% to 22.55% compared to the previous month. The cost per conversion saw a significant drop, decreasing from $22.23 to $8.70. Additionally, cost per click (CPC) decreased from $2.04 to $1.96.  
  
The campaign also experienced a 7.50% improvement in click-through rate during this period.  
  
In terms of keyword performance, "car repair shops near me" and "car repair memphis tn" generated the highest traffic, resulting in a combined total of 19 conversions.  
  
Overall, March was a highly successful month, with strong gains in both engagement and cost-efficiency. We’ll continue to optimize around top-performing keywords and strategies to maintain this momentum.

**What did we do during the month?**

* We updated the landing page to improve user experience and better align with the ad messaging, helping drive higher engagement and conversions.
* Ad performance was monitored, and continuous adjustments were made based on data and insights to continually improve campaign performance.
* We added new negative keywords based on search term reports to filter out irrelevant traffic and improve overall ad efficiency.

**What's Next?**  
  

* We'll focus on bid strategy optimizations.

* Negative keywords will be added based on recent search term data to minimize irrelevant traffic and improve overall campaign efficiency.
