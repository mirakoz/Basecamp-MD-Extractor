### The Looker Studio Report & Insights - January 2025

Posted by Yeliz Kolasinli on Feb 6, 2025

[![Yeliz Kolasinli](https://asset1.basecamp.com/2325709/people/18747394/photo/avatar.96.gif "Yeliz Kolasinli")](/2325709/people/18747394)

Hi Kevin,  
  
You can find the Looker Studio Live Report below;  
  
[https://lookerstudio.google.com/u/3/reporting/f78455b6-2b67-4a3f-997b-50f5d…](https://lookerstudio.google.com/u/3/reporting/f78455b6-2b67-4a3f-997b-50f5d9e6c3b5/page/p_2lp46xqnmd "https://lookerstudio.google.com/u/3/reporting/f78455b6-2b67-4a3f-997b-50f5d9e6c3b5/page/p_2lp46xqnmd")  
  
**What are the main insights for the month?**

* We spent $204, which resulted in 5 phone calls and 1 form submission. The conversion rate was 5.88% and the unit cost was $34.04.

  

* The keywords that generated the highest traffic were "car repair shops near me." and "car repair memphis tn" Together, these keywords led to a total of 6 conversions.

* The cost per click (CPC) decreased by 33% in January.

  

**What did we do during the month?**

* Ad performance was monitored and continuous adjustments were made based on data and insights to continually improve campaign performance.

* Negative keywords have been added based on search term results.

**What's next?**

* We'll focus on bid strategy optimizations.

* We'll continue with ongoing search term optimizations.

Yeliz
