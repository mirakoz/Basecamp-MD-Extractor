**What are the main insights for the month?**  
  

In February, our campaign achieved notable results, receiving a total of 6 phone calls and 3 form submissions. The conversion rate increased by 56% compared to the previous month. The cost per conversion saw a significant drop, decreasing from $34.04 to $22.23.

The campaign also experienced a 13% improvement in click-through rate during this period.

In terms of keyword performance, "car repair shops near me" and "car repair memphis tn" generated the highest traffic, resulting in a combined total of 8 conversions. Furthermore, the keyword "auto repair memphis tn" generated 1 conversion.  
  

**What did we do during the month?**

* Ad performance was monitored and continuous adjustments were made based on data and insights to continually improve campaign performance.

* Negative keywords have been added based on search term results.

  
  
**What's next?**  

* Once the campaign learnings are complete, we will switch our campaign bidding strategies to Maximize Conversions to optimize them towards conversions.
