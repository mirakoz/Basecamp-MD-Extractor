**What are the main insights for the month?**  
  

In June, our campaign delivered strong results, achieving a total of 19 conversions, the highest in the past 3 months. These included 8 direct calls from ads and 11 calls from the website.

We also saw notable improvements in key performance metrics:

* CTR increased from 5.56% to 6.29%, indicating stronger ad engagement.

* Conversion rate improved by 13.2%, reflecting better-qualified traffic.

* Impressions grew by 85% compared to May, showing broader reach and increased visibility.

From a keyword performance perspective, “car repair memphis tn” and “car repair near me” stood out as top performers, driving the highest traffic and contributing 16 of the total conversions.

These results suggest that our current strategy is resonating well with our target audience, especially around high-intent keywords.  
  
**What did we do during the month?**  
The budget was updated from $200 to $500.  
  
We added new negative keywords based on search term reports to filter out irrelevant traffic and improve overall ad efficiency.  
  
**What's needed?**

Lead quality feedback is very important for campaign optimization. We need feedback from the client regarding qualified leads.
