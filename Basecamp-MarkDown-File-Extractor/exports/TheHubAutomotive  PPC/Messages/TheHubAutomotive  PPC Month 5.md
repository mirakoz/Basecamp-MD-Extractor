**What are the main insights for the month?**

In April, our campaign generated 13 conversions, which included 6 calls from ads, 7 calls from the website.

The click-through rate (CTR) increased from 5.63% to 6.10%, indicating that ads are becoming more engaging and relevant to users.

We also saw a 3.70% reduction in cost per click(CPC), bringing it down from $1.96 to $1.89, which indicates improved cost-efficiency.From a keyword perspective, “car repair shops near me” and “car repair memphis tn” were top performers, driving the highest traffic and generating 10 conversions.

Additionally, the keyword “mechanic memphis tn” delivered 2 conversions.  
  

**What did we do during the month?**

* Ad performance was monitored, and continuous adjustments were made based on data and insights to continually improve campaign performance.
* We added new negative keywords based on search term reports to filter out irrelevant traffic and improve overall ad efficiency.

  
**What's needed?**  
Lead quality feedback is very important for campaign optimization. We need feedback from the client regarding qualified leads.
