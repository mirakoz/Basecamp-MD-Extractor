**What are the main insights for the month?**

In August, the campaign generated 24 conversions, including 4 form submissions, 12 calls from ads, and 8 calls from the website.  

Click-through rate (CTR) improved from 5.47% in July to 6.2% in August, showing that ads became more appealing and effective at capturing user interest. Despite a slight decrease in impressions, clicks increased from 230 to 233.

Average CPC stayed almost the same ($2.16 vs. $2.14), indicating consistent cost control while gaining higher engagement.  
  
  

**What did we do during the month?**

We added new negative keywords based on search term reports to filter out irrelevant traffic and improve overall ad efficiency.  
  

**What's needed?**

Lead quality feedback is very important for campaign optimization. We need feedback from the client regarding qualified leads.
