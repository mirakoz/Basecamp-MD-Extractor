**Mirak Ozoglu**   

Hi Armando and Trevor,

Thanks for sending the mockups and the raw images over! The "Under $500K" messaging for the new single detached bungalows is a great hook. I also received the clean images without the graphic overlay and will make sure to crop them accordingly for Google Ads.

Regarding the campaign structure and budget allocation, I agree with Trevor that taking a full 20-25% away from our currently successful campaigns might disrupt the great momentum we've built. Since the client wants to ensure the other live campaigns are not negatively impacted, here is my recommendation for the most efficient setup:

* **Meta Ads:** Since the target audience for these bungalows is downsizers and retirees, we shouldn't build a completely new campaign from scratch. Let's inject these new creatives as a new ad set directly into our existing "Retirees" campaign. This allows us to leverage the algorithm that is already getting us amazing CPLs, and we can softly allocate about 15% of the budget toward these specific ads to test the waters.
* **Google Ads:** I recommend adding these new cropped images and bungalow copy as a new "Asset Group" within our highly successful Performance Max campaign. PMax is already doing the heavy lifting for our conversions, so feeding it this new offering will yield the best results without needing a separate budget.

This approach allows us to launch the new bungalow offering using the existing Markdale budget without shocking the system or starving our current lead flow.

If you're both good with this strategy, we’ll get the images cropped and the new assets launched across the platforms asap. Let me know!
