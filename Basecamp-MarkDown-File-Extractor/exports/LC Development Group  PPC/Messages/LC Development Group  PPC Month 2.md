Hi Trevor,

My apologies for the slight delay on this! Your **March 2026 PPC Performance Report** for LC Development Group is now ready.

[https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1ejCpVuRwnyfx-zzerrP7np9HdrK8xyrOeEG…](https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1ejCpVuRwnyfx-zzerrP7np9HdrK8xyrOeEG2OdLmNQ8/edit?usp=sharing "https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1ejCpVuRwnyfx-zzerrP7np9HdrK8xyrOeEG2OdLmNQ8/edit?usp=sharing")

Here are some key results from March:

* **Combined Performance:** We had a fantastic month overall, generating **54 total leads** across all platforms with a total spend of $4,837.29.
* **Google Ads:** The Google campaigns generated 24 leads , with the "Home Buyer Insights" Performance Max campaign being the absolute standout, driving 20 of those leads at a very efficient $58.72 CPA.
* **Meta Ads Highlight:** The Meta campaigns had an incredibly strong debut! In just the first month, we spent $483.35 and generated **30 leads** at a highly efficient Cost Per Lead (CPL) of just $16.11.

**Monthly Insights & Actions:**
The biggest win this month was getting the Meta Ads up and running alongside a bulletproof tracking system. The Meta campaigns have officially exited the learning phase, and we are seeing a perfectly balanced audience response (16 Retirees / New Families and 14 First-Time Buyers ), proving our tailored messaging is hitting the exact right demographics. Additionally, the backend lead sheet you shared in mid-March has been incredibly helpful for us to monitor lead quality and optimize our targeting.

**What's Completed in the Previous Month?**

* **Meta Ads Launched:** Successfully built and launched the Meta Ads campaigns utilizing specific angles for Retirees ($125k savings) and First-Time Buyers ($389,900 entry point).
* **Campaign Optimization:** We switched the "Performance Max - Home Buyer Insights" campaign to a "Maximize Conversions" bidding strategy to remove CPA limitations and boost performance. We also added new negative keywords based on the latest search terms.
* **Full Tracking Overhaul:** Completed a deep-dive technical setup via Google Tag Manager for the new Markdale Meadows landing page. We successfully integrated Jotform submission tracking, as well as micro-conversions (phone numbers and mailto clicks).
* **GA4 Architecture:** Set up a dedicated GA4 data stream for the landing page to keep the project's traffic isolated and clean from the main LCDG website.
* **Billing & Pacing:** Resolved the initial Meta billing threshold/flagging issues and established strict daily guardrails to ensure the budget paces evenly throughout the month without dark periods.

Please let us know if the client has any questions about the data or the backend sheet columns.

If you have time this week, we would love to schedule a quick call to discuss the report in more detail and review our next steps for April. Please let me know your availability!
