**Here are some key results from February:**

* We've tracked lead form submissions and website phone calls as primary conversions.
* Overall Spend: $1,157
* Total Conversions: 2 conversions at a CPA of $578.39
* Despite the first month, our campaigns had a good start with high CTR similar to the industry benchmarks. We expect better results in the following months.

**Monthly Insights & Actions:**

* The campaigns look promising, having started with a high CTR and already performing better in the first couple of days of March.
* We expect further improvement as the awareness impact of the Performance Max campaign takes effect and with the launch of Meta Ads.

  

**What's Completed in the Previous Month?**

* Google campaigns were launched on February 12.
* 31 negative keywords were added.
* We conducted an A/B test for the Performance Max campaign to compare different audience targeting approaches, such as location based interests, to see whether it would be effective in reaching people interested in Markdale who live outside our targeted location. However, the test was unsuccessful due to the number of unqualified leads we observed in the backend. As a result, we continued with the more strictly targeted campaign.
